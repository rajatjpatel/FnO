## 3. The Golden Rules of Execution

1.  **Risk Management First:** Never enter a trade where the "Max Loss" is more than 2-3% of your total capital. Even a 90% probability trade can fail.
2.  **Liquidity is Life:** Only trade strategies on stocks or indices with tight bid-ask spreads. If you can't get out, the payoff chart doesn't matter.
3.  **The Greeks are the Compass:** Before you enter, check your **Delta** (Direction), **Theta** (Time), and **Vega** (Volatility). If you are fighting all three, you are fighting the market.
